Take a short rest? During a short rest, your character takes a 5 minute where they can; stand guard, sit in place, ride on a wagon or other vehicle, or do other tasks that don't require much exertion, to gain the following benefits:

  • Renew all the uses of your encounter powers, and regain the use of your second wind and action points.
  • Spend Healing Surges to regain hit points.
